Energized positive change through intentional activity

Everyday is a fresh start

I believe in the energy created through coaching and consulting when I am enabling the ambitious to expand their perspective by breaking apart complex ideas and identifying challenges. I am here to support you through your change as a trusted advisor. Let's chat about what your next focus will be and how those achievements will be accomplished.

Weekly Coaching for Change

Weekly 30-minute coaching calls with Sophia that are aimed to amplify perspective, boost confidence and erase self-doubt. These sessions will focus on the change needed for a goal you set.

  • Your desire to reach the goal
  • Identifying any knowledge needed to attain the goal
  • The ability to reach your goal
  • The power to sustain the goal once it is accomplished

Monthly Coaching with Accountability

A monthly 60-minute coaching call with Sophia geared towards turning your ideas into actionable plans. This service provides accountability check-ins between sessions via email.

These sessions will:

  • Identify what needs to be accomplished, the timeline to reach the goal, who will support the effort and what resources are available.
  • Capture the major milestones and challenges towards accomplishing the goal.
  • Create a mindset of success and list out tasks for each milestone.
  • Form a detailed action plan to support your goal.
