Workshop plus Coaching

I lead a 3 hour workshop, in a group setting, that is aimed at defining what future success looks like to you. I bring together like-minded ambitious professionals to amplify their perspective so that they can clearly see what is hindering their progress. A one-time complimentary coaching session is included in this service package and will be scheduled within 30 days of the workshop.

These sessions will:

  • Identify what needs to be accomplished, the timeline to reach the goal, and what resources are available.
  • Capture the major milestones and challenges towards accomplishing the goal.
  • Create a mindset of success and list out tasks for each milestone.
  • Form a detailed action plan to support your goal.
